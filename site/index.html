About Me

Hi, I'm Gabi!
Brazilian animal lover
in Eindhoven 🇧🇷🇳🇱

I grew up surrounded by animals and have loved them my entire life. I've had dogs, and today I share my home with my husband and our son — and two wonderful cats, Amora and Panqueca, who bring joy to every single day.

Living in the Eindhoven area, I noticed how stressful it can be to leave your beloved pet behind. That's what inspired me to turn my lifelong passion for animals into a service that truly makes a difference — for both pets and their owners.

Every animal I care for gets the same warmth and attention I give my own — because to me, it's never just a job. It's a privilege to be trusted with someone's furry family member.

Services

What I offer

Tailored care for cats and small dogs in the Eindhoven area, provided in the comfort of your home or on a refreshing walk.

For Cats

Home Visits

I come to your home so your cat stays in their own safe environment. Each visit is a full, attentive session — not a quick drop-in.

  • Feeding & fresh water
  • Litter box cleaning
  • Playtime & interactive games
  • Behavioural & health check
  • Medication administration if needed
  • Cuddles & affection on their terms
  • Photo/video update sent to you
For Dogs

Dog Walking

Your dog deserves more than a quick potty break. I offer engaging walks that stimulate body and mind, with the same loving attention I give every animal.

  • 30- or 60-minute walk options
  • Exploration & sniff time encouraged
  • Positive reinforcement approach
  • Post-walk feeding if requested
  • Behavioural observation & update
  • Photo/video update sent to you

Fees & Extras

Simple & transparent pricing

One straightforward rate — no surprises. Weekdays, weekends and school holidays are all the same price.

🏡

Home Visit / Dog Walk

€21/visit

Per visit, within a 5 km radius of Winkelcentrum Woensel, Eindhoven. Same rate on weekdays, weekends and during school holidays.

  • Weekdays — €21
  • Weekends — €21
  • School holidays — €21
📍

Outside the 5 km zone

+€1/km

If you live outside the 5 km radius from Winkelcentrum Woensel, a small travel surcharge applies per additional kilometre.

  • €21 base rate
  • + €1 per km beyond 5 km
  • Contact me to check your area
🎉

Dutch National Holidays

+€10.50/visit

Visits on official Dutch national holidays carry an additional holiday surcharge on top of the regular rate.

  • €21 base rate
  • + €10.50 holiday surcharge
  • Total: €31.50 on national holidays
💡

Not sure if you're in the 5 km zone? Just send me a WhatsApp or email with your address and I'll check right away. The meet & greet is always free, with no obligation.
